About Skyworks

Connecting people, places, and things through innovative semiconductor solutions

Our Story

Skyworks Solutions is a leading global developer of integrated circuits (ICs) that connect people, places, and things. We design, develop, manufacture and market analog and mixed-signal semiconductors that enable high-performance applications in the wireless and broadband markets.

Founded in 2001 through the consolidation of the wireless infrastructure and wireless systems businesses of Conexant Systems, Inc., Skyworks has grown to become a premier supplier to global original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) serving the home, office, industrial, automotive, medical, and military/aerospace markets.

Today, Skyworks serves customers globally from offices located in North America, Europe, Asia, and elsewhere. Our global workforce brings together talented individuals from diverse backgrounds and cultures, united by a passion for innovation and excellence.

Mission Statement

To deliver innovative, high-performance analog semiconductors that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Vision Statement

To be the world's most trusted partner for analog semiconductor solutions, enabling the connected world of tomorrow.
